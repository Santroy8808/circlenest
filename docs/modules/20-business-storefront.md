# Business Storefront

## Purpose

Give Professional accounts a public-facing business presence.

## User-Facing Surfaces

- Business profile.
- Public storefront.
- Inquiry/contact flow.

## Primary Code Areas

- `src/modules/business-storefront`
- `src/components/business`
- `src/app/production-zone/business`
- `src/app/storefront`

## Data Ownership

- future business profile, storefront, inquiry, verification tables.

## Core Workflows

- Create business profile.
- Publish public storefront.
- Receive external inquiries.
- Prepare for future email linking.

## Access Rules

Professional accounts only. Admin can verify/hold/reject.

## Integrations

Market, jobs, ads, admin, mail.

## Current Design Notes

Public storefront must have explicit external boundary rules.

## Smoke Checklist

- Storefront visible externally.
- Business edit requires owner.
