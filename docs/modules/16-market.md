# Market

## Purpose

Provide The Market as a clean marketplace of clickable product/service thumbnails.

## User-Facing Surfaces

- Market listing grid.
- Listing detail.
- Create listing wizard.

## Primary Code Areas

- `src/modules/market`
- `src/components/market`
- `src/app/market`

## Data Ownership

- future market listing and static category tables.

## Core Workflows

- Browse thumbnails.
- Open detail page.
- Contributor limited listings.
- Professional unlimited listings and storefront handoff.

## Access Rules

Free browses only. Contributor can list within limits. Professional unlimited.

## Integrations

Ads, business storefront, media, reports, production zone.

## Current Design Notes

No user-created categories. Ads are separate placements, not inside listing bodies.

## Smoke Checklist

- Free sees no create controls.
- Listings are square thumbnails with title and price.
