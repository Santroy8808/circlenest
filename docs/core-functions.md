# Theta-Space Core Functions

Core functions are the baseline product surface every Free account must be able to use.

## Free-Tier Core Functions

1. Posting on the main Stream, including replies and reactions.
2. Creating groups and posting inside joined groups.
3. Direct messages and group messages.
4. Market listings and job listings. Free members may publish up to 3 Market listings in each rolling 14-day period, with up to 3 photos per listing.
5. Gallery upload, tagging, filtering, commenting, and deletion.

## Contributor and Paid/Expanded Functions

Contributor adds expanded storage, capped Market tools, group moderator tools, and Writers Corner. Contributor does not add business profiles or identity switching, Jobs, Events, Fundraisers, general ad creation, or auditor-profile creation. Paid and special tiers may add business profiles and identity switching, higher limits, advertising, fundraisers, events, Writers Corner, auditor profiles, mass mail, Org-only profiles, larger storage, or moderation eligibility. Free members may browse the Auditor Directory, but auditor-profile creation is Coming Soon and is not part of Free access.

Unavailable capabilities must be hidden from the user's menus, pages, tutorials, and direct API responses rather than displayed as disabled upgrade gates.

The Free tier must not lose the core functions above when paid-tier features change.
